Data gridder

Sit back and lets do the heavy Typing for you

1

Create a new poll

A poll is a design of the data you would want to collect from your audience, select the new poll option in your dashboard to create a new poll.

2

Collect data

After creating your poll, distribute the poll code or poll link to your audience. The poll code is used in the "Find a Poll" section of this page, while the poll link allows direct access to the poll by pasting it into any preferred browser.

3

View data

Once users have registered for your poll, you can view the data in your poll through the "My Polls" option on your dashboard. Please note that this is only a preview of your data and does not reflect the actual appearance of your document.

4

Retrieve data

To retrieve your poll in document format, simply click the download button. You have the option to choose a custom name for the downloaded document. Once you click download, the file will begin downloading automatically.
